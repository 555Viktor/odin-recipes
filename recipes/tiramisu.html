Delicious Tiramisu

Tiramisu is a classic Italian dessert that features layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers and a rich mixture of mascarpone cheese, eggs, and sugar. Itâ€™s often dusted with cocoa powder and can be garnished with chocolate shavings. The name "tiramisu" means "pick me up," referring to the energizing effects of coffee.

Ingredints:

  • 1 cup strong coffee, cooled
  • 2 tablespoons coffee liqueur (optional)
  • 3 large eggs, separated
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup mascarpone cheese
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 24 ladyfinger cookies
  • Cocoa powder for dusting
  • Dark chocolate shavings (optional)

Preparation:

  1. In a bowl, mix cooled coffee with coffee liqueur (if using).
  2. In another bowl, beat egg yolks and sugar until creamy, then fold in mascarpone.
  3. In a separate bowl, whip heavy cream and fold into the mascarpone mixture.
  4. Dip ladyfingers in coffee mixture and layer them in a dish.
  5. Spread half of the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers.
  6. Repeat layers and finish with remaining mascarpone.
  7. Chill for at least 4 hours, then dust with cocoa powder before serving.
