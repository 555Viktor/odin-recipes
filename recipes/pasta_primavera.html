Delicious Pasta Primavera

Ingredients:

  • 12 ounces pasta (e.g., penne or fettuccine)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 medium bell pepper (red or yellow), sliced
  • 1 medium zucchini, sliced
  • 1 medium carrot, sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

    • Preparation:

    1. Cook the Pasta: Boil salted water, cook pasta until al dente, drain, and set aside.
    2. Sauté the Vegetables: Heat olive oil in a skillet, add minced garlic, and sauté for 30 seconds. Add bell pepper, zucchini, and carrot; sauté for 3-4 minutes.
    3. Add Broccoli and Tomatoes: Stir in broccoli florets and cherry tomatoes; cook for another 3-4 minutes.
    4. Combine Pasta and Vegetables: Add cooked pasta to the skillet, sprinkle with oregano, salt, and pepper. Toss to combine.
    5. Serve: Divide into bowls, garnish with basil and Parmesan if desired.
    6. Enjoy: Serve warm!
